Custom  Designed  Furniture

I strive to continue the long tradition of American east coast furniture crafting with its quality and timeless appeal. I use the latest in production technologies, complemented with time-honored techniques and hand-tooled details. My fine home and office furnishings are all custom-crafted and finished to exceed expectations.

About Seth

Seth discovered his interest in creative arts very early, but not until graduate school did he recognize that his real passion was for designing furniture and crafting solutions. He prefers to use walnut, oak and cherry, hardwoods of the Appalachian forests he explored in his youth, but has worked with exotic woods as well.

Process

A dialog between client and woodworker is integral to Seth’s design process. Initial consultation delineates the client’s wishes, where a piece is going, how it will be used, and who will be using it. Inspired by the discussion, Seth then develops a design which also meets requirements of size, function and budget. Consultation with the client continues with consideration of particular elements, and only after this does the build begin. Each step, from lumber selection to final delivery, is then recorded in a journal which will be included with the final delivery of the piece.
